Video.js

The industry standard open-source HTML5 video player

About Video.js

Video.js is the most widely used open-source HTML5 player, powering video on millions of websites. It's built from the ground up for an HTML5 world and supports all modern streaming formats.

Key Features

  • Extensive plugin ecosystem with 100+ official and community plugins
  • HLS and DASH streaming support
  • Highly customizable with themes and skins
  • Excellent accessibility features (WCAG compliant)
  • Framework agnostic - works with React, Vue, Angular, etc.
  • Mobile-first responsive design
  • DRM support (Widevine, PlayReady, FairPlay)
  • Active development and large community

Best For

Enterprise applications, production environments, projects requiring extensive customization, streaming services, and situations where plugin support is important.
